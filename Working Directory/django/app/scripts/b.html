Kindly find the detailed information about the e-Yantra Lab Setup Initiative (eLSI) as below:

e-Yantra – a project in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), IIT Bombay - is a flagship project of MHRD through the National Mission on Education through ICT (NMEICT) to spread Embedded systems and Robotics education in colleges across India. e-Yantra Lab Setup Initiative (eLSI) is a holistic approach to

  • Impart theoretical and programming knowledge to teachers through workshops
  • Provide hands-on experience to teachers through an online Task Based Training (TBT)
  • Help colleges set up their own Robotics labs.

We have established robotics labs in {{count}}+ colleges across India.
